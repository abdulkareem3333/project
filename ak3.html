Worksheet: Past, Present, and Future Tenses

Part 1: Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb in brackets.

1. Yesterday, I (play) football with my friends. (played)
2. Sarah usually (read) a book before she goes to bed. (reads)
3. We (watch) a movie right now. (are watching)
4. They (talk) about their holiday plans. (were talking)
5. By this time next year, I (graduate) from college. (will graduate)
6. The children (swim) in the pool at this time yesterday. (were swimming)
7. He (cook) dinner when I called him. (was cooking)
8. Next weekend, they (visit) their grandparents. (will visit)
